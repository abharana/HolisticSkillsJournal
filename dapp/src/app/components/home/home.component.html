Holistic Skills Journal inspires users to indulge in a habit of recording their life skills and reflecting on their holistic growth.

IQ(Intelligence Quotient), EQ(Emotional Quotient), SQ (Social Quotient), CQ (Curiosity Quotient), AQ (Adaptability Quotient)
The App allows you to perform the following activities.

1. Register your Metamask Account providing a friendly username to your Ethereum account.
2. Capture a Skill that you have most recently acquired and exhibited.
3. Share an link to users who can approve of your newly acquired skills.
4. Approve skills for users with a feedback.
5. View List of Skils acquired across different categories.

Over a period of time, users of the dapp, will gather a plethora of skills often categorised into one of the below categories. The journal provides a reflection on their holistic development and helps the individuals focus on their shortcomings.

I was inspired to work on this application based on research articles from https://www.legofoundation.com/.
